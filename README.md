### Technical specifications

* Display: scalable from 640x480
* Audio: 2 music channels, 4 sound effect channels, 1 speech channel
* Circuit clock: 60Hz
* Code: Wren, Lua
* Gamepad: 6 buttons (D-Pad + A/B)
* Keyboard and mouse: supported
